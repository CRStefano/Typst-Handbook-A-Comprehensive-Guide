# Typst: From ZERO to HERO
The (almost) complete manual to master Typst.

Tired of fighting with LaTeX or losing your mind over formatting in Word? Welcome to the future of technical writing.

This manual is your ultimate companion to go from "I don't even know what markup is" to creating complex documents, elegant thesis papers, stunning resumes, and high-level presentations.

"This entire document was written in Typst. Yes, I used nothing else. If you see any typos... well, appreciate the effort!"
— Stefano Coelati Rama

## What you will find in this Handbook
From setting up your first Hello, Typst! document to building complex academic templates and automations:

Parts I-II: Fundamentals, formatting, images, tables, and mathematical formulas (yes, writing math has never been this fun).

Parts III-IV: The core of the language: variables, logic, loops, and the magical set and show rules.

Parts V-VI: Advanced layout, modules, and community packages.

Parts VII: Real-world use cases (Resume templates, Thesis, Slides).
